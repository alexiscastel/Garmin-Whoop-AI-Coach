🧯 3) Monotony + Strain: The Hidden Driver of Breakdown Risk (and How to Fix It)

Your single most concerning week-level marker is:

  • monotony_7d 2.34 and strain_7d 1866.8 on 2026‑03‑08—both period highs.

Why monotony matters for you:
Even with some volatility across the whole 57 days, your peak week looks like it was comprised of multiple similarly demanding days (rather than clear hard/easy separation). In endurance athletes, that often produces:

  • lingering heaviness,
  • persistent “medium fatigue,”
  • and higher probability that a small niggle turns into forced downtime.

Fix conceptually: keep the weekly total similar, but change the shape:

  • Make easy days truly easy (so they function as recovery and allow quality days to be higher quality).
  • Keep only 2–3 “meaningful stress” days per week (quality + long run), and actively reduce load on the other days.
