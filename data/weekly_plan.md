### Intensity Zones (use as the “language” for the next 28 days)

**Running (anchored to LTHR ≈ 174 bpm; trail/heat can shift pace—prioritize HR + RPE):**
- **Z1 Recovery:** ≤ **147 bpm** (≤85% LTHR) | RPE 1–2
- **Z2 Easy Aerobic:** **148–155 bpm** (85–89%) | RPE 2–3  
  - **Execution guardrail (from your data):** on easy days aim **~135–145 bpm** when possible (treadmill/flat), even if pace feels “too slow.”
- **Z3 Steady / Marathon-ish:** **156–164 bpm** (90–94%) | RPE 4–5
- **Z4 Threshold / Cruise:** **165–172 bpm** (95–99%) | RPE 6–7
- **Z5 VO2 / Speed:** **≥173 bpm** (≥100%) | RPE 8–9 (for short strides/sprints use **feel/power + form**, not HR)

**Readiness guardrails (apply daily):**
